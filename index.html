About Us

Welcome to EEC. Easwari Engineering College was instituted in the academic year 1996-1997, with the Approval of Government of Tamil Nadu and AICTE, New Delhi. As per Govt., Regulations for affiliation, originally the college was affiliated to the University of Madras, subsequently the college is affiliated to Anna University Chennai since 2002.
